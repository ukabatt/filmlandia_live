Our Podcast

Welcome to our delightfully derpy yet thoughtful podcast! Here, we chat about whatever movie or TV show catches our interest, from big-budget blockbusters to binge-worthy series to movies we loved as kids (and maybe still do!).


Listen in as we blend movie analysis with tales of family shenanigans and the rollercoaster of growing up. Expect equal parts giggle fits and whoa-that-was-deeper-than-expected moments as we unpack the stories that shape us.
